Strategy Information

Moving Average Crossover with Volatility Filter

This bot implements a classic Moving Average Crossover strategy with an added volatility filter to avoid trading during low-volatility periods.

  • Buy Signal: When the 5-period MA crosses above the 20-period MA and volatility is above threshold
  • Sell Signal: When the 5-period MA crosses below the 20-period MA and volatility is above threshold
  • No Trading: When market volatility is below the minimum threshold (currently 0.0002)
